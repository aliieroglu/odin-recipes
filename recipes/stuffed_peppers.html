Stuffed Peppers

Stuffed peppers are a classic dish made by hollowing out bell peppers and
filling them with a mixture of meat, rice, vegetables, cheese, and spices.
Baked in the oven until tender and filled with a warm and flavorful filling,
stuffed peppers are a delicious and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 4 bell peppers (any color)
  • 1 lb ground beef or turkey
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cut the tops off of the bell peppers and remove the seeds and membranes.
  3. In a large pan, cook the ground beef or turkey over medium heat until browned. Drain any excess fat.
  4. Add the onion to the pan and cook until soft, about 5 minutes.
  5. Add the cooked rice, diced tomatoes, garlic powder, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper to the pan and stir to combine.
  6. Fill each bell pepper with the meat and rice mixture.
  7. Place the stuffed peppers in a baking dish and bake for 35-40 minutes, or until the peppers are tender.
  8. Sprinkle the shredded cheese on top of the stuffed peppers and return to the oven for 5-10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  9. Serve hot and enjoy!
